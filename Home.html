Makhni Chicken Handi Recipe:

“Makhni” in Pakistan literally means “buttery” having been derived from the Urdu word for Butter called Makhan.But don’t be fooled.
Pakistani Chicken Makhni tastes nothing like the sweet Butter Chicken you’ve probably tried in the West because it is not sweet.
Makhni is a handi that is made with simple steps

INGREDIENTS:

  • Chicken (Boneless, cubed) ½ kg
  • Yogurt 1 cup
  • Ginger Garlic Paste 1 tbsp
  • Red Chili Powder 1 tsp
  • White Cumin (Roasted, ground) 1 tsp
  • Onion Paste 2
  • Salt To taste
  • Lemon 2
  • Green Chilies Paste 3
  • Coriander Seeds 1 tbsp
  • Black Peppercorns 1 tsp
  • Oil ½ cup
  • Butter 2 tbsp

METHODS:

  1. Heat 1 cup oil in a handi and add 3 raw and ground onions.
  2. Fry till they turn slightly pink.
  3. Now add ½ kg boneless chicken, 1 tsp turmeric, salt and 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste.
  4. Cook till the water in the handi is compleltly dried.
  5. Now add 4 finely chopped tomatoes, 1 tbsp crushed red chili and 1 tsp ground all spice.
  6. Cook well then add 1 tsp crushed black pepper,
  7. Add 1 tsp roasted and ground cumin and lemon juice.
  8. Leave the handi on dum and add butter before covering the lid.
  9. After 5 minutes add 2 tbsp finely chopped ginger
  10. Add 6 green chilies and 1 bunch coriander leaves. Serve hot
